Colors

Colours are visual perceptions that result from the way light interacts with objects and human eye. There are many different colours, and they can be broadly categorized into primary, secondary and tertiary colours.Primary colours are red, blue and yellow. They are not created by mixing colours.Secondary colours such as green, orange and purple are created by mixing two primary colours.Tertiary colours such as yellow-green,red-orange and blue-purple are created by mixing one primary colour with one secondary colour.
